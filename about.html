About the Artist

Exploring identity, heritage, and contemporary expression through mixed media and textile traditions

Asemahle Ntlonti is a contemporary South African artist whose work bridges the gap between traditional African textile practices and modern artistic expression. Born and raised in the Eastern Cape, her art is deeply rooted in the cultural heritage and storytelling traditions of her community.

Working primarily with mixed media, Ntlonti creates layered compositions that speak to themes of identity, memory, and place. Her use of materials such as acrylic gel, cotton, and leno thread alongside traditional paint creates textural narratives that invite both visual and tactile engagement.

Her practice is informed by a deep understanding of how landscape and cultural identity intersect, creating works that serve as both personal reflections and broader commentaries on contemporary South African experience.
